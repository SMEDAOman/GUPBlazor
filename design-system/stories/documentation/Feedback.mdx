<Meta title="Feedback" />

There's a lot of ways you can pass your feedback to the library development team.

1. Drop us an email at [gup_pmo@mtcit.gov.om](mailto:gup_pmo@mtcit.gov.om)
2. Join the [Whatsapp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/KqN9RuWxKaW1nTXUXm8I6p)
3. Join the Teams chat (please request access via email)

When reporting a bug, please try to either reproduce in the Storybook by modifying controls in a story or by forking our [Codepen playground](https://codepen.io/certainlyakey/pen/LYKxrjw) and reproducing it there (click a small "Fork" button at the right bottom of the screen).
